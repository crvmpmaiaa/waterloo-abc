Who We Are

More Than a Gym

Waterloo ABC is a grassroots amateur boxing club rooted in the heart of Liverpool's Waterloo community. We're not a fancy fitness chain — we're a proper boxing gym where dedication, discipline, and community come first.

Whether you're a complete beginner lacing up gloves for the first time, a parent looking for a positive outlet for your kids, or an aspiring competitor chasing championship belts — there's a place for you here. Our members range from juniors to adults, and everyone trains with the same respect and work ethic.

With affordable sessions from just £3 to £5 a class, we believe world-class coaching shouldn't come with a premium price tag.
